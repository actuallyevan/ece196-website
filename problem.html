Problem Definition & Motivation

Indoor environmental conditions such as poor air quality, noise, and lighting frequently impair the cognitive performance of students and knowledge workers, while unoccupied rooms with active lighting and HVAC systems result in unnecessary energy waste.

Being students ourselves, we are often in classrooms that may be uncomfortable or distracting. This can be particulary disruptive during exams or presentations that require your full focus. We wanted to address this problem by, at the bare minimum, providing an easy and comprehensive solution to monitor indoor conditions. Additionally, we all are interested in IoT, embedded, and programming, and had similar ideas for our project proposals.

Current Solutions

Our team is developing a low-cost, modular IoT solution that combines acoustic sensors, machine learning, and a cloud-based analytics platform to detect and localize water leaks in real-time before they become major issues.

By focusing on non-invasive installation and leveraging existing infrastructure, our solution minimizes deployment costs while maximizing impact. Early testing shows our system can detect leaks with 95% accuracy and reduce detection time from weeks to hours.
