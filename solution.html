High level sketch of our solution

What it Does

Our solution is an indoor environmental monitoring system that tracks multiple metrics, including:

  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Gas (VOCs)
  • Carbon Dioxide
  • PM 1, 2.5, 4, 10
  • Light
  • Sound

This will give the end user a full picture of their indoor environment + AQI. This information will be visible on a display for easy viewing and monitoring. Multiple display views will be available to the user.
